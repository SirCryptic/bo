Ice Staff Checklist

  • Obtain the Monocle: During Round 8, a Shock Mimic will spawn. Killing it will drop a Monocle.
  • Destroy the Purple Lanterns: Shoot the purple lanterns around the map to guide them to the Tombs or Neolithic Catacombs. This will reveal Roman numeral symbols (I, II, III, IV, etc.).
  • Complete the Roman Numeral Puzzles: In the Tombs, find the bull mural. Shoot the symbols in numerical order to create the Taurus constellation. Completing the puzzle triggers a lockdown event. Afterward, the Staff part will spawn.
  • Complete the Second Puzzle: In the Neolithic Catacombs, shoot the symbols in the right order. Completing this will unlock the Ice Staff head.
  • Complete the Ice Staff: Go to the Dark Aether Nexus and interact with the altar. This will trigger a lockdown event where you must protect the staff from Zombies. Once successful, the Ice Staff will be available for pickup.

Now you have the Ice Staff!

Final Boss Fight

Phase 1

The final boss is located in the Dark Aether near the stone structure in the back of the area. After completing the statue challenges, a bridge will lead you to the boss battle.

Phase 2

Use only Pack-a-Punch Tier 3 weapons or legendary gold weapons to deal damage to the boss. Wonder Weapons will not work.

Phase 3

Survive the boss fight to get your rewards!
